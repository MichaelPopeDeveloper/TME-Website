About Us

The Founders of TME-Systems and a Core Group of Change Advocates have spent countless years perfecting a Social Enterprise Business Model that Directly Addresses Social and Economic Inequality Specifically in Disenfranchised Communities.

We are not Business as Usual. Creating Social and Economic Systems that have an Immediate Impact is our Main Focus. Innovation is our Motto. Our Directive is Clear.

We Design Social and Economic Systems, that Utilize Existing Resources, large or small, to Create Infrastructure and Revenue Development. In other words, are there ENOUGH Resources (money, people, infrastructure, etc.) currently in these Communities, to REBUILD THEMSELVES?

Revenue Development Opportunities for Individuals, Organizations and Businesses are all part of our Common Sense Solution Plan. This IMMEDIATELY Increases Revenue Flow into local Communities and supports Long Term Sustainability.

Disenfranchised Communities Already have the numbers (140 million affected) and the Spending Power (Trillions of Dollars in 2016)! Common Sense Dictates that Communities Take Advantage of those 2 ASSETS!

The National Task Force was created in Response!

TME-Systems consists of a vast Network of Business Leaders, Organizations and Thousands of Ordinary People, Participating to do Extraordinary Things that Impact their Community!
